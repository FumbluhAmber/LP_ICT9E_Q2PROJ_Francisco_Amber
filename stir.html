≋Stir-fried ≋Mollusks≋

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs Cooked Mussels
  • 12 Fresh Thai Chilies (or according to taste)
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Small Shallot (sliced)
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • A Handful of Thai Sweet Basil

Seasoning Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Seasoning Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Roasted Chili Paste (Prik Pao)
  • 1 tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 1 tsp MSG

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Aromatics: Slice the shallot and garlic. Chop the Thai chilies according to taste.
  2. Heat the Oil: In a pan, heat the vegetable oil and sauté the garlic, shallots, and chilies until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Mussels: Add the cooked mussels to the pan and stir them in with the aromatics.
  4. Add the Seasoning: Pour in the oyster sauce, soy sauce, roasted chili paste, fish sauce, and MSG. Stir to combine.
  5. Add Water: Pour in the 1/4 cup of water and allow the mussels to cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Finish with Basil: Stir in the handful of Thai sweet basil just before serving.
  7. Serve: Serve the dish with Jasmine rice for a complete meal.
