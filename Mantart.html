【Fruit Tart with Egg Custard】

≋Zesty Fruit-Topped Tart≋ Creamy Egg Custard Base

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 Large Red Bell Peppers (for the crust)
  • 4 Eggs
  • 450g Heavy Cream
  • 50g Mozzarella
  • 2 Potatoes (optional)
  • Crab Meat (optional)
  • 1/2 Apple (for the filling)

Instructions:

  1. Preparing the Crust: Slice the red bell peppers into halves and remove the seeds. Roast them until they become soft and slightly charred, creating a crust for your tart.
  2. Preparing the Egg Custard: In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, mozzarella, and the diced apple. For a savory twist, you can also add crab meat or grated potatoes into the mix.
  3. Baking the Custard: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Pour the custard mixture into the bell pepper crust and bake for 30-40 minutes or until the custard has set and turned golden brown.
  4. Adding the Toppings: While the tart is baking, prepare your fruit toppings. Slice cherry tomatoes, olives, and cambray onions. Once the tart is out of the oven, top with fresh slices of grapes, tangerines, and the cherry tomatoes.
  5. Finishing Touches: Allow the tart to cool slightly before slicing and serving. The crispy bell pepper crust pairs beautifully with the rich, creamy custard and vibrant fruit toppings.
