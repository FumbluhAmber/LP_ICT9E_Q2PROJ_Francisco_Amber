Ingredients:

  • 300g Bread Flour or 150g 00 pizza flour and 150g all-purpose flour
  • 10g Sea Salt
  • 2g Active Dry Yeast or Instant Yeast
  • 200g Lukewarm Water
  • 4g Olive Oil
  • 1 cup Extra flour for dusting
  • 4-6 Slices Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Garlic, minced
  • 1/4 Onion, thinly sliced

Instructions:

  1. Making the Pizza Dough: In a large mixing bowl, use your finger to stir together the lukewarm water, yeast, and olive oil. Add in your flours and salt. Mix with your hand until you get a shaggy dough. Cover with a kitchen towel and rest for 15 mins.
  2. Prepare Dough: On a floured surface, knead the dough until smooth. Cut into 4 equal pieces and shape them into balls. Let them rise in a bowl or tray covered with a damp towel for 3-4 hours (if baking same day) or refrigerate overnight for up to 3 days.
  3. If Freezing: Store dough in mini ziplock bags with olive oil, and thaw before use.
  4. Prepping the Pizza: Preheat the oven to 550°F/290°C (or as hot as your oven can go). Shape the dough on parchment paper. Stretch it out with your fingers to the desired size.
  5. Baking the Pizza: Bake the pizza for 9-15 mins, checking the crust and toppings to your liking. Slide it onto a baking stone or tray. Use the middle rack for even heat distribution.
  6. To Serve: Ensure all ingredients are fully cooked. Serve immediately for the best flavor and texture.
