【Barometz Balut】

≋Red ≋and ≋Bright Mutton≋

Ingredients:

  • 500 g Tomatoes
  • 4 Lamb Chops
  • 1 Can of Crab (with soup)
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Red Wine
  • Olive Oil
  • Young Bracken in Water
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Marinate Lamb Chops: Marinate lamb chops with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  2. Prepare Tomatoes: Boil tomatoes lightly, peel the skin, cut into chunks, and remove the seeds.
  3. Brown the Lamb Chops: Brown lamb chops in a grooved pan with olive oil until browned. Pour red wine over the meat and steam for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Cook Tomatoes: Remove the meat and add tomatoes to the pan. Simmer tomatoes with the leftover sauce.
  5. Add Crab: Add the canned crab and its soup to the tomatoes. Stir in tomato paste to thicken the sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Finish the Dish: Place the lamb meat back into the sauce and simmer until well combined.
  7. Serve: Serve the lamb on the sauce and side with boiled young bracken.
