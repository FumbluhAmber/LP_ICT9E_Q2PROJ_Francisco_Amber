≋Hot-smoked ≋Fire-breathed≋ ≋Juicy≋ ≋Roast≋

Ingredients:

  • 4 slabs of Thick-cut beef steaks (Ribeye or Strip)
  • Freshly ground Black Pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp Butter (per steak) - optional
  • Herbs like rosemary or thyme - optional

Instructions:

  1. Prepping Steak: Generously salt and pepper steak on all sides and place on a wire rack tray. Cover in cling wrap and refrigerate overnight. Let steaks thaw for 30-45 mins before cooking.
  2. Cooking Steak in Oven (with probe thermometer): Preheat oven to 250°F (120°C). Cook the steaks until the internal temperature reaches 125°F (for rare).
  3. Cooking Steak in Oven (without probe thermometer): Preheat oven to 125°F (50°C). Cook the steaks for 45 mins (gets it to rare).
  4. Cast Iron Browning Steak: Heat a cast iron pan with 1 tbsp of neutral oil on high heat. Form a crust on both sides of the steak (1-2 mins per side). Optionally, add butter for extra richness. Let the steaks rest before cutting.
  5. Red Wine Reduction: In the steak pan, sauté shallots until translucent. Add 1 cup of red wine and 1 cup of beef stock. Stir until the sauce is reduced. Add herbs like rosemary or thyme for extra aroma.
  6. To Serve: Make sure the steak is cooked to at least rare (internal temp 125°F). Slice the steak and drizzle with red wine reduction.
  7. Bonus Way to Eat: Slice leftover steaks and store them. Reheat in an air fryer or microwave and serve with rice seasoned with furikake, mirin, and rice vinegar.
