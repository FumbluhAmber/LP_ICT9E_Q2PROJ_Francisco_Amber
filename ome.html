【Mandrake, Big Bat, and Basilisk Omelette】

≋Savory & Hearty Protein-packed Omelette≋

Ingredients:

  • 5 eggs, cracked and whisked
  • 2 pieces of thick cut bacon, sliced into lardons
  • 2 Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and finely diced
  • 1/2 tsp Knorr Chicken Bouillon Powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Bacon: In a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, cook the bacon until crispy and golden. Remove and set aside, leaving the bacon drippings in the pan.
  2. Prepare the Potatoes: Add the diced Yukon Gold potatoes and seasonings (bouillon powder, garlic powder, cayenne, paprika, chili powder) to the pan. Cook for about 5 minutes until the potatoes are crispy on the outside and soft inside. Season with salt to taste. Remove from the pan and combine with the cooked bacon.
  3. Cook the Eggs: In the same skillet, add the beaten eggs. Reduce heat to medium and cook until the eggs are mostly set, then flip.
  4. Fill the Omelette: Once the eggs are flipped, add the potato and bacon mixture. Cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute, ensuring the filling is heated through.
  5. Finish and Serve: Once cooked, slide the omelette out of the pan, roll it around the filling, slice in half, and serve hot.
