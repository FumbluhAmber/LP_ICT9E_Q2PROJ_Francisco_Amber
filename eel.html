【Grilled Giant Parasite】

≋Kabayaki Style≋

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Mirin
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1.5 oz Sake
  • 1/2 cup Rice Vinegar
  • 1/3 cup Dark Brown Sugar
  • 4 eel fillets (Unagi)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a saucepan, combine mirin, soy sauce, sake, rice vinegar, and brown sugar. Bring to a boil and simmer for about 5 minutes until the sauce thickens. Stir constantly to prevent boil over.
  2. Prepare the Eel: Slice the eel fillets into 4-inch pieces. Using a brush, coat both sides of each piece with the unagi sauce.
  3. Grill the Eel: Preheat your broiler to high (500°F). Place the eel on the broiler pan and broil, flipping and reapplying the sauce regularly. The sauce will bubble and caramelize after each application.
  4. Repeat the Grilling: Repeat the process of broiling and reapplying the sauce about six times. Let the edges crisp up at the end.
  5. Serve or Store: Serve immediately or refrigerate for later use. To reheat, cook the eel skin-down in a pan with a touch of oil over gentle heat.
  6. Serving Suggestion: Serve your grilled unagi over rice for a classic, delicious meal!
