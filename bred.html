【﻿Plundered Bread】

≋Smooth Fresh-out Buns≋

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm whole milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 Tablespoons granulated sugar, divided
  • 1 large egg at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup butter (preferably unsalted), softened and cut up
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or if you're using salted butter 1/2 teaspoon)
  • 3 cups bread flour

Instructions:

  1. Activate Yeast: In the bowl of a stand mixer, pour the milk and add the instant yeast and 1 tablespoon of sugar. Allow to stand for 5 minutes until the yeast is nice and bubbly.
  2. Mix Dough: With the mixer on low, slowly add in 1/3 of the flour at a time until everything is incorporated, occasionally scraping down the sides.
  3. Knead Dough: Turn the speed up to medium (4-5) and allow to knead in the stand mixer for about 5 minutes.
  4. Prepare for Rising: Grease a large bowl with a little olive oil and wipe away the excess. Once the dough is done kneading, turn it out into the bowl and rotate a few times to make sure all sides are covered with oil.
  5. Let Dough Rise: Cover and let rise in a warm area for 90 minutes until the dough has doubled in size.
  6. Prepare Pans: Grease the sides of 2 8" round cake pans (or your chosen baking dish) and cut parchment paper for the bottom.
  7. Form Dough Balls: Once the dough has risen, flour a work surface and turn the dough onto it. Cut into 16 equal pieces, and roll them into balls. Space them out evenly in the pan and allow to rise one more time for 30 minutes.
  8. Preheat Oven: While the dough is rising, preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  9. Bake: Bake for 25 minutes. Remove from the heat, brush with butter, and sprinkle with salt.
  10. Serve: Serve the bread warm!
