【Mixed Sorbet Swirl】

≋Fruity & Refreshing Swirl of Flavors≋

Ingredients:

  • Simple Syrup: 2 cups granulated sugar, 2 cups water
  • Pineapple Sorbet: 10oz frozen pineapple, 1/3 cup simple syrup, 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • Mango Sorbet: 10oz frozen mango, 1/3 cup simple syrup, 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • Mixed Berry Sorbet: 10oz frozen mixed berries, 1/3 cup simple syrup, 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • Blueberry Sorbet: 10oz frozen blueberries, 1/3 cup simple syrup, 1/2 tsp lemon juice, 1 tsp spirulina (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Make Simple Syrup: Combine 2 cups sugar and 2 cups water in a saucepot. Stir and heat until the sugar dissolves. Bring to a boil for 2 minutes. Let cool.
  2. Pineapple Sorbet: Blend frozen pineapple, simple syrup, and lemon juice until smooth. Optionally, add a drop of purple food coloring and blend.
  3. Mango Sorbet: Blend frozen mango, simple syrup, and lemon juice until smooth.
  4. Mixed Berry Sorbet: Blend frozen mixed berries, simple syrup, and lemon juice until smooth.
  5. Blueberry Sorbet: Blend frozen blueberries, simple syrup, and lemon juice until smooth. Add spirulina or food coloring if desired.
  6. Swirl the Sorbet: Spoon dollops of each sorbet into a 9x13 pan. Swirl them together using a skewer or knife. Press plastic wrap into the sorbet and freeze for at least 3 hours.
  7. Serve: Once frozen, scoop and serve immediately, or let sit for 1-2 minutes to soften slightly.
