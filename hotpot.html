【Huge Scorpion and Walking Mushroom Hotpot】

≋Spicy, Savory, and Hearty Hotpot≋

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg white
  • 250g shrimp
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 tbsp xiao xing wine
  • 3 bell peppers
  • Napa cabbage
  • Shimeiji mushroom
  • Dried kelp (for broth)
  • Anchovy tablets (optional)
  • 4-5 tbsp miso paste
  • Soy sauce (to taste)
  • Shirataki noodles (optional)
  • Potatoes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Broth: In a pot, simmer the dried kelp in boiled water for 20 minutes (or up to 3 hours for a richer flavor). Remove the kelp when done.
  2. Prepare the Shrimp: Mince the shrimp until it becomes mushy. In a mixing bowl, combine the shrimp with corn starch, xiao xing wine, egg white, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Prepare the Vegetables: Cut the shimeiji mushrooms in half, then slice them vertically. Slice the bell peppers and chop the napa cabbage into burrito-sized wraps. Optionally, peel and chop the potatoes.
  4. Cook the Hotpot: In a pot, add the prepared shrimp mixture and allow it to cook. Then add the potatoes, bell peppers, cabbage, shimeiji mushrooms, and shirataki noodles. Simmer over medium heat for about 10 minutes.
  5. Flavor the Broth: After removing the kelp, add the miso paste (4-5 tbsp) and anchovy tablets to the broth. Stir well to combine.
  6. Finish the Soup: Add soy sauce to taste, then bring the soup to a gentle simmer. Once the ingredients are well-cooked and the flavors have melded, your hotpot is ready to serve!
