【Hippogriff Soup Dumplings】

≋Tasty ≋Soaked≋ ≋Dumpling≋

Ingredients:

  • Soup Gelatin:
    • Hot Water
    • Hot Chicken Stock
    • Soy Sauce
    • Gelatin Powder
  • Soup Dumpling Wrapper:
    • All-Purpose Flour
    • Warm Water
    • Salt
  • Filling:
    • Ground Pork
    • Sesame Oil
    • Soy Sauce
    • Ginger
    • Sugar
    • Salt and Pepper
    • Rice Vinegar
    • Green Onion
    • Chinese Black Vinegar

Instructions:

  1. Make Soup Gelatin: Mix stock and gelatin in hot water, dissolve and cool to set.
  2. Make Dough: Combine flour, warm water, and salt, knead, and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Prepare Filling: Mix ground pork, sesame oil, soy sauce, ginger, sugar, salt, pepper, rice vinegar, and green onion.
  4. Assemble Dumplings: Roll dough, fill with pork mixture and soup gelatin, fold and seal.
  5. Steam: Steam dumplings in a basket for 8 minutes using an instant pot or traditional steaming method.
  6. Serve: Enjoy with Chinese black vinegar for dipping.
