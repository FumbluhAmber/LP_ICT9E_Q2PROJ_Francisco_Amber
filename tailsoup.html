Ingredients:

  • 1 Oxtail aka dragon tail
  • 1-2 pack Beef Bone Broth
  • Handful Radish, chopped
  • 1/4 large Onion
  • 1 tbsp Garlic, minced
  • Green Onion garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepping the Oxtail: Rinse oxtail well with water to remove blood and bone fragments. Cut off the excess fat and place oxtail in clean water while prepping other ingredients. Change water frequently.
  2. Boiling the Oxtail: Bring a small pot of water to a boil. Once boiling, add the oxtail and cook for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes, rinse oxtail and clean the pot.
  3. Prepping Veggies: Quarter and thinly slice a palm-sized amount of radish. Mince garlic and thinly slice onion.
  4. Cooking the Soup: Return the pot to the stove, add beef bone broth, oxtail, garlic, onion, and radish. Cover and bring to a rolling boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours until the meat is tender.
  5. To Serve: Ensure all ingredients are fully cooked. Serve with hot rice and side dishes like kimchi. Enjoy!
